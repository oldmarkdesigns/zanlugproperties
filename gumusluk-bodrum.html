Gümüşlük, BODRUM

A sun-drenched hillside villa overlooking the Aegean, where whitewashed architecture meets soft stone terraces and bougainvillea.

The Gümüşlük residence is designed for long Mediterranean days: shaded outdoor lounges, an infinity pool facing the sea and generous sliding doors that dissolve the boundary between interior and exterior. The interiors blend local stone, natural wood and traditional Turkish craftsmanship, creating spaces that feel both contemporary and deeply connected to place.
