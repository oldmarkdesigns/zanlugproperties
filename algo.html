Älgö, SALTSJÖBADEN

A serene waterfront retreat tucked into the Stockholm archipelago, where pine forests meet open sea and long summer evenings blend into soft Nordic light.

This Älgö residence balances Scandinavian minimalism with warm, natural materials. Generous glazing opens towards the water, while calm, muted interiors keep the focus on the horizon. Large social areas, multiple terraces and a seamless indoor–outdoor flow make this home ideal for both slow weekends and larger gatherings.
