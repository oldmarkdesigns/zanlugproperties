Hede, HÄRJEDALEN

A contemporary mountain lodge framed by birch trees and open views towards the Härjedalen peaks.

The Hede home is crafted for all seasons: open fireplaces for winter, generous glazing for low winter sun and deep terraces for long, bright summer nights. Natural timber, wool and stone create a tactile interior that connects directly to the surrounding landscape.
